My Opinion of Core Skills:

I was surprised by the fact that everything we were learning was basically common sense, or at least they were in my household. They were things that my parents were always telling me whenever I encountered problems or moving forward in life. But what was challenging was trying to apply them to life consciously or in the moment. Because I would just get so caught in the moment and get bad tunnel vision but I think with time the aspects from core will come easily.

I think we are focusing on core learning because it is a huge part of what companies are looking for in potential employers. Core is a lot of human skills and problem solving skills and I think that's important to have. Someone could be an excellent coder but be a complete dick and nobody wants that in their work space. Companies are looking for likable, skilled and problem solving people.

I think the time spent on core is important because it helps set people up for the work space we are going in. It teaches us to realize we are human and we can still learn and grow our minds. For some this is completely foreign and for others it's a good a reminder
