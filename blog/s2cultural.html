Learning plan!

Strengths

  • Focused
  • Work well under pressure

Limitations

  • Procrastination
  • Social

When I start working on something I become very focused and it's very hard to break this. I can go for hours before my brain eventually needs a little break to take care of basic human needs and even then jumping back into work isn't hard. This can be countered by my procrastination though, this only occurs when I'm not sure in what I'm suppose to be doing, or I'm not confident in what I'm doing. During the procrastination mode if people socialize with me my brain no longer wants to do work and will do anything to keep the conversation going, which furthers the procrastination. I do however work really well under pressure as I have been taught not to react and to take control of the situation as best as I can to reach my end goal. This is in both high stress and time constrained situations.

The Commitment!

I'll commit myself to doing bursts of EDA work with bursts of life tasks in-between, this will prevent any procrastination or distractions. I will be sure to juggle in-order of urgency. I'll seek help from my facilitator or fellow students after exhausting all resources on the internet and self attempts, if that time exceeds half an hour. I'll do so by using the slack communication app or email if possible. I'll be sure to fulfill my work duties and make sure I have my EDA work under control before taking on anything extra in both work and social aspects. To combat my procrastination I'll push myself to do short burst of work and work my way up in length of time.

What I Expect:

I expect the facilitation team to give me answers to questions I have regarding the course and to help me out as much as they can when I encounter a problem. The reason behind this is because I have more than likely already googled it in a million different ways and have come up dry. I expect them to be understanding of any situations I find myself in and to offer nothing but kindness with obvious exceptions.
